Our Story

Born under the Oslo moon in 2021, Mocha Moon Cafe began as a dream — a dream to create a space where night owls, creatives, and coffee lovers could gather and feel at home.

Our passion lies in the little things: slow-steeped brews, fresh pastries, and conversations that stretch into the evening hours. Whether you’re journaling, studying, dating, or decompressing — you’re always welcome at Mocha Moon
